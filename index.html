Kitesurf



Kitesurfing is a thrilling water sport that combines surfing with wind power. Riders use a large, controllable kite to glide across the water on a board, delivering an adrenaline-pumping experience for all skill levels.
  • Requires wind speeds of 10-25 knots for optimal conditions
  • Combines elements of surfing, wakeboarding, and paragliding
  • Suitable for both calm waters and ocean waves
  • Develops strength, balance, and body control
  • Can be enjoyed by beginners through professional athletes
  • Popular destinations include coastal beaches and windy lakes worldwide
